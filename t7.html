Core Developers

Core Developers is a software house, established in 2015, located in Karachi – the commercial capital of Pakistan. Core Developers provides a host of services to its clients ranging from IT support operations to developing complete systems with IQMS documentation. Technology used to achieve this is a combination of robust IBM content management tools and Microsoft development environments such as .NET & Structured Query Language.Verticals served by Paperless Technology are primarily pharmaceutical and financial institutions. Its portfolio includes GlaxoSmithKline, Bayer, Roche, Merck, Wyeth, Standard Chartered Bank, American Express, HSBC, Dubai Islamic Bank, Habib Bank and MCB Bank Ltd.

In order to cater to their multinational clientele, Core Developers has added to their core competencies in the shape of training and development of their staff on latest technologies and adopting methodologies to standardize software development techniques.

Also, Core Developers established strategic business alliances with the foremost names in the industry, such as IBM and Microsoft to further their outreach. This process entailed development of business solutions that meet rigorous quality control criteria established both by IBM and Microsoft.

Moreover, clients using these business solutions provide detailed feedback in order to complete the certification and partnership process.
